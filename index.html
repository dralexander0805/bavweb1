About Blue Angels Virtual

Welcome to Blue Angels Virtual! Operating on VATSIM with support for MSFS and X-Plane, we bring precision, discipline, and excellence to virtual flight. While not yet an official VATSIM Special Operations Affiliate (VSOA), we are actively working toward this goal.

Why Fly With Us?

  • Realistic formation flying
  • Regular practice sessions and public airshows
  • Open to MSFS and X-Plane pilots of all skill levels
