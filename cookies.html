Cookie Policy

Transparency in how we use cookies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help improve user experience by remembering your preferences, enabling website functionalities, and analyzing site performance.

How We Use Cookies

At Blue Angels Virtual, we use cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic and performance using Google Analytics.
  • Remember your preferences to enhance user experience.

We do not use cookies to store personal information or share your data with third parties.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, such as navigating pages and accessing secure areas.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to collect anonymous data on site usage to help us improve functionality and performance.

Your Cookie Choices

When visiting our site, you can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. For more detailed instructions, refer to your browser's help documentation.

Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Analytics, which may place cookies on your device to analyze site usage. All data collected is anonymized, and we do not share this information with others.

GDPR Compliance

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data collected via cookies.
  • Request the deletion of your cookie data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

If you have questions or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at support@blueangelsvirtual.org.
