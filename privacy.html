Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Our Commitment

At Blue Angels Virtual, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your privacy is respected. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with GDPR regulations.

What We Collect

We may collect limited data when you interact with our website, such as your IP address and usage data, through the use of Google Analytics. This helps us analyze site traffic and improve our services. All data collected is anonymized.

How We Use Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. The most we do is use Google Analytics to gain insights into website usage patterns.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data.
  • The right to restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • The right to data portability.

Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure that your data is protected against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Data is stored securely and only accessed by authorized personnel.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@blueangelsvirtual.org.
